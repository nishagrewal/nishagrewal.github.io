Research

Cosmology studies the evolution and characteristics of the Universe. To better understand the history of the dark sector, we measure statistics from galaxy catalogs and maps to constrain cosmological parameters that describe dark matter and dark energy. Statistics measured from gravitational lensing (the bending of light around large masses) are effective in probing the growth of large-scale structure and the accelerated expansion of the Universe.
