About Me

I am a PhD student at the University of Edinburgh, supervised by Dr Joe Zuntz. My research centers on the applications of higher-order statistics (HoS) in weak lensing cosmology. I am a member of the Legacy Survey in Space and Time (LSST) Dark Energy Science Collaboration (DESC).
